Spline Search Demo

A piecewise cubic Hermite spline anchored at (0, 1) and (1, 0). Each control point carries a measured slope (tangent). Click anywhere on the curve to drop a new control point with a random, parity-aligned tangent. Tangents whose sign opposes the segment secant are reflected (shown in orange).
